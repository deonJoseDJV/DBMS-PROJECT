How It Works

Adopting a pet is a rewarding experience. Here's our simple three-step process.

Search for a Pet

Browse available pets based on your preferences. You can filter by species, breed, age, size, and location to find the perfect match for your home and lifestyle.

Start Searching

Contact the Shelter

Get in touch with the shelter or rescue organization. Schedule a visit to meet your potential new pet and learn more about their temperament, health, and specific needs.

Find Shelters

Complete the Adoption

Finalize paperwork and welcome your new family member! Most shelters will require an application, possibly a home check, and an adoption fee that helps cover medical costs.

Adoption FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to adopt?

Requirements vary by shelter, but generally include being at least 18 years old, having identification, proof of residence, and sometimes landlord approval if you rent. Some shelters also conduct interviews or home checks.

How much does it cost to adopt?

Adoption fees typically range from $50 to $300 depending on the animal and organization. These fees help cover vaccination, microchipping, and spay/neuter costs.

How long does the adoption process take?

The timeline varies, but you can typically expect the process to take anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on the shelter's requirements and how quickly they can process your application.
